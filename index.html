REMEMBERING MH370

MH370 Chronology

Malaysia Airlines Flight 370 was a scheduled international passenger flight operated by Malaysia Airlines that disappeared on 8 March 2014 carrying 12 crews and 227 passengers while flying from Kuala Lumpur International Airport in Malaysia to its planned destination, Beijing Capital International Airport

  1. March 8, 2014
    • 12.41 am - MH370 flight departure from KLIA
    • 1.19 am - Final communication from cockpit "Good night, Malaysian Three Seven Zero"
    • 2.15 am - The last point detects radar flight MH370
  2. March 15, 2014
    • Prime Minister Datuk Seri Najib Tun Razak announced that the plane appears to have been flown for hours
  3. March 18, 2014
    • The Australia led search in the Indian Ocean
  4. March 24, 2014
    • Prime Minister Datuk Seri Najib Tun Razak announced that MH370 was presumed crached in the Indian Ocean
